Our Story

Night Roast began with three lifelong friends — Ace, Lloyd, and Gino — who shared one ritual: sitting together over a cup of coffee, talking about life, dreams, and everything in between.

One night, gathered around a small table much like the one in the picture, the idea sparked: “Why don’t we build our own coffee shop?”

With Ace’s creativity, Lloyd’s craftsmanship, and Gino’s passion for flavor, they built a place that captures the warmth of their friendship — a café where stories, laughter, and good coffee never run out.

Today, Night Roast stands as a tribute to friendship, late-night conversations, and the belief that the best ideas are brewed together.
